About me

Analyst. Storyteller. Bridge Builder.

I'm a Data and Business Analyst with an MSc in Information Technology with Business Intelligence from Robert Gordon University Aberdeen — built on three years at Solartail Company where I designed end-to-end data pipelines, built Power BI dashboards for non-technical stakeholders, and presented executive-level insights to senior leadership.

My background is deliberately hybrid. I've worked at the intersection of data engineering and business strategy — eliciting requirements from stakeholders, translating them into scalable analytics solutions, and delivering findings that influenced real decisions. I'm equally at home writing SUMPRODUCT formulas in Excel and building ETL pipelines in Python.

My MSc thesis explored intelligent business analysis using free-tier LLMs — a framework for multi-industry decision support that sits at the centre of everything in this portfolio. These projects are not exercises. They reflect how I actually think about data, business problems, and the tools that connect them.
